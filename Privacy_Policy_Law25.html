Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 09, 2025

1. Introduction

We place great importance on protecting your personal information. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how you can exercise your rights in compliance with Quebec's Law 25.

By accessing our website (bunkers.co),(jprovo.st) or using our cybersecurity services, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Collection of Personal Data

We collect certain information in the following cases:

  • When you use our site (Google Analytics)
  • When you book an appointment (Calendly)
  • When you use the form using (formcarry.com)
  • When using our AI-powered cybersecurity services

The information collected includes:

  • Name, email address, and phone number (via Calendly).
  • IP address, device type, visited pages (via Google Analytics).
  • Security-related data (suspicious login attempts, identified threats) used to continuously improve our AI-based protection.

3. Purpose of Data Collection

We use this data to:

  • Analyze and improve user experience on our site.
  • Manage appointments and professional interactions.
  • Enhance our cybersecurity systems through artificial intelligence.
  • Ensure data protection by detecting and preventing cyber threats.

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for commercial purposes.

4. Use of Artificial Intelligence and Data Anonymization

Our solutions use advanced artificial intelligence technologies to analyze threats and automate anomaly detection. All collected data:

  • Is anonymized before processing.
  • Is encrypted and protected against unauthorized access.
  • Cannot be directly linked to a client to ensure confidentiality.

Our system relies on a network of sensors and the use of proprietary and open-source technologies to monitor and visualize threats in real-time.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary for the purposes for which it was collected:

  • Browsing data: 12 months (Google Analytics).
  • Appointment data: 24 months (Calendly).
  • Security logs and AI analysis: up to 36 months before full anonymization.

6. Your Rights Under Law 25

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to access and rectify: You can request a copy of your data and correct any errors.
  • Right to deletion: You can request the removal of your personal information.
  • Right to object: You can refuse certain uses of your data.
  • Right to data portability: You can request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

To exercise your rights, contact us at: security@bunkers.co

7. Security and Data Protection

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or loss, including:

  • Advanced encryption for sensitive data (AES-256).
  • Secure storage with restricted access.
  • Continuous monitoring and logging of access.

8. Billing and Personal Data

For billing management, we use the Zoho Invoice platform, a service provided by Zoho Corporation. Your personal information required to issue invoices, such as your name, address, email address, and transaction details, may be processed and stored on Zoho's servers. This provider is subject to industry-standard security measures and operates internationally. A contractual framework is in place to ensure the protection of your information, in accordance with the requirements of Bill 25.
